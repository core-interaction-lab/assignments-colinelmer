Assignment 1: What if the instagram algorithm prioritized artists and image content?

The instagram algorithm is constantly changing, but the current iteration largely focuses on video content and posts from your mutuals. This makes it extremely difficult for artists on instagram to be succesful or even have their posts seen by their followers. The instagram feed prioritizes big corporations and short, easily digestible videos, but in this "what if" scenario, we see the effect of an algorithm that is in favor of small creators.
